Hi, I’m Ben Swift—I’m a hacker, digital artist and computer science lecturer at the Australian National University in Canberra, Australia. I lead the code/creativity/culture group, a new research group within the Research School of Computer Science. The c/c/c group exists to open up spaces for discussion about way that code is shaping flows of information/culture/humanity in today’s (and tomorrow’s) world. You can read more about the c/c/c group at their website.

This is my personal homepage & blog, where I can wax lyrical about the projects I’m involved in, the students I’m supervising, and the thoughts in my head.

If you’d like to get in touch, then get in touch on Twitter or send me an email on ben.swift@anu.edu.au.
