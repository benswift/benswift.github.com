Bio

I’m a Canberra lad, born and bred. One day I may have to move away from this place, but for now I’m here and quite happy about it.

I have one wife, one kid, zero pets, three bikes and four guitars. They all make me happy (in monotonically weakly decreasing order).

On the weekends I ride my mountain bike, play football (soccer), watch the footy (afl) and cricket and go to church. I’m also trying to learn Mandarin so that I can better communicate with my in-laws. Ask me how I’m going with that in a year.
