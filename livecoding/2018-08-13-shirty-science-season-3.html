Shirty Science Season 3 launch party

Shirty Science is an ongoing arts/science collaboration; scientists get paired up with artists to make a sweet t-shirt explaining/showing off their research.

So of course Kieran and I were pumped when they invited to perform at their Season 3 launch party at Doughnut Dept.—our very first livecoding set together. I did music, he did visuals, and we mixed our screens together using our new Roland V1-HD video mixer. It was super cool.

Sadly, no photos videos at present—although I’m trying to track some down (if you were there and have some, get in touch).
