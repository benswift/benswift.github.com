CECS Welcome Party 2019

As usual, CECS kicked off the year with a welcome party for students & staff old and new. Kieran and I were invited back this year to do a 90min livecoding set—I’m making the music in Extempore, he’s making the visuals by writing OpenGL shaders. It was great to be invited to show what’s possible we reimagine what humans and computers can do together.
